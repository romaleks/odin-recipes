Watermelon Feta Salad

Description

This is a great salad. With the watermelon the kids will like it also.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons white balsamic vinegar
  • Â½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • 8 ounces grape tomatoes, halved
  • 3 cups arugula
  • 1 cup sliced red onion
  • 2 pounds watermelon, cubed
  • 4 ounces feta cheese, cut into 1/4-inch cubes

Steps

  1. Whisk together the olive oil, vinegar, and salt in a large mixing bowl.Whisk together the olive oil, vinegar, and salt in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Add the tomatoes, arugula, and onions; toss to coat.
  3. Gently stir in the watermelon and feta cheese to serve.
